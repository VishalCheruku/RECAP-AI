RECAP AI - Core Idea and Functionality

RECAP AI is a discharge follow-up intelligence system for reducing avoidable hospital readmissions. It turns patient documents into a clear risk report, routes care reminders, and escalates patients who need follow-up support.

Core workflow:
1. The user uploads a discharge summary, prescription, PDF, image, TXT, or JSON file.
2. OCR and document parsing extract the patient name, age, diagnosis, medical history, phone route, medicines, and discharge date.
3. The local clinical AI risk engine reads the extracted text and predicts LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH readmission risk using age, diagnoses, medicines, prior admissions, adherence concerns, and urgent warning signs.
4. Demo patient policy supports the panel scenario:
   - Asha Devi: HIGH risk
   - Ravi Kumar: MEDIUM risk
   - Meena Joseph: HIGH risk
   - Vikram Singh: LOW risk
   - Surya: HIGH risk
   - Vikas: HIGH risk
5. Every generated report sends an SMS reminder.
6. Only HIGH and MEDIUM risk reports trigger a call workflow.
7. Vikram is LOW risk, so he receives SMS monitoring but no call.
8. Surya routes SMS and calls to +917013864843.
9. Every other report routes SMS and calls to +91996667724.
10. The frontend shows call progress while the call request is active, then confirms that the message was relayed through SMS and call.

Data storage:
RECAP AI saves demo patients, report records, SMS logs, call logs, family alerts, doctor alerts, and medication replies into MongoDB database recap_ai when MongoDB is running locally. This gives the panel team live records to inspect in MongoDB Compass.

OCR and prescription support:
The document extractor handles selectable PDFs, scanned PDFs, images, plain text, and JSON. For prescriptions, it looks for common formats such as Patient, Age, Dx, C/O, Rx, medicines, and advice sections, then infers disease and risk from both diagnosis words and medication clues.

Clinical output:
The report presents the patient summary, routed contact, AI risk score, risk factors, SMS/call action, and system activity timeline. It is generated only after the Analyze button is pressed.
